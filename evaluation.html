Evaluation

My EPQ explored the impacts that artificial intelligence has on our society and while there were some challenges along the way, this project allowed me to improve upon many skills and expand my knowledge on the topic of AI’s current impact on the world.

The research that I did for the discussion has given me substantial knowledge on artificial intelligence and while I do wish that my research was more extensive in some parts, the research that I did do taught me how to identify relevant sources and only focus on the key information.

Making my project as an artefact, I had to learn how to work on both the content and the website at the same time and be able to allocate my time in such a way that both parts got the attention they needed. This helped me improve my time management and organisational skills and allowed me to go back and refine my skills of making a website from scratch.

Overall, I’m glad with how this project has turned out. Though I do have some regrets about not broadening my discussion or sources, there are many valuable skills that I have gained from working on this project for the last couple of months that I will forever be grateful for.
