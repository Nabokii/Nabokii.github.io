Abstract

This study, presented as a website, examines the role of artificial intelligence (AI) in human society, weighing its potential benefits and drawbacks. Firstly, the discussion will explore the positives and the sectors in society where using AI would be useful, drawing attention to things such as enhanced problem solving and technological advances. These points will then be contrasted with the concerns of using AI, including aspects like safety, bias, and privacy. The research will then conclude that AI can be a valuable tool with the correct precautions while offering suggestions to mitigate associated risks and maximise societal benefits.
